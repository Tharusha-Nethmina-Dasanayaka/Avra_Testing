Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-02-13

This Privacy Policy explains how Avra (“Avra”, “we”, “us”, or “our”) collects, uses, stores, and shares information when you use our Omnichannel Merchant Portal and related services (the “Service”).

1) What we collect

Depending on how you use Avra, we may collect:

  • Account & identity information (e.g., name, email, internal user/tenant identifiers).
  • Channel connection data needed to connect WhatsApp / Messenger / Instagram, such as:
    • Meta App identifiers and configuration references
    • Business Account / Page / WABA identifiers (where applicable)
    • Access tokens or system-user tokens (stored encrypted)
  • Message & event metadata (e.g., webhook event IDs, timestamps, delivery status).
  • Optional content you submit (e.g., templates, automated replies, customer messages) as required for the Service.
  • Technical data (e.g., IP address, browser type, logs for debugging and security).

2) How we use information

  • To provide and operate the Service (connect channels, process webhooks, send/receive messages).
  • To secure the Service (fraud prevention, abuse detection, access control).
  • To troubleshoot issues and improve reliability and performance.
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms.

3) How we store and protect data

  • Encryption: Sensitive credentials (e.g., access tokens) are stored encrypted at rest.
  • Access control: Data is separated by tenant/workspace, and access is limited to authorized users.
  • Logging: We avoid logging sensitive secrets. Where logs exist, they are minimized and used for operations/security.

Note: No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we implement reasonable safeguards appropriate to the sensitivity of the data.

4) Sharing of information

We may share information only as needed:

  • With Meta Platforms (Facebook/Meta) via their APIs to enable WhatsApp/Messenger/Instagram functionality.
  • Service providers (hosting, databases, monitoring) solely to run the Service under confidentiality obligations.
  • Legal / compliance if required by law, subpoena, or to protect rights and safety.

We do not sell your personal data.

5) Data retention

We retain information only for as long as necessary to provide the Service and for legitimate business purposes (e.g., security, audits, dispute resolution), unless a longer period is required by law.

6) Your choices and rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or export your data.

To request access or deletion, contact us using the details below or visit our Data Deletion Instructions.

7) Cookies

Avra may use cookies or similar technologies for session management, security, and basic analytics. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

8) Children’s privacy

The Service is not intended for children under 13 (or the age required by local law). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
