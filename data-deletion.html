Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: 2026-02-13

Avra provides a way for users and businesses to request deletion of data associated with their use of the Service, including data obtained through Meta integrations (WhatsApp/Messenger/Instagram) where applicable.

How to request deletion

  1. Send an email to: privacy@avra.example
  2. Use the subject line: Data Deletion Request
  3. In the email body, include:
    • Your Avra account email (or merchant workspace name)
    • Your Meta Business / WABA / Page identifiers (if known)
    • Which channels to delete (WhatsApp / Messenger / Instagram)

Replace the email above with your real support/privacy email before publishing.

What we delete

After verifying the request, we will delete (or anonymize where appropriate):

  • Stored channel credentials/tokens (encrypted secrets)
  • Channel linkage records (business/page/waba mapping)
  • Webhook subscription references tied to your workspace (where applicable)
  • Optional stored message logs/content if your workspace has them enabled

Timeframe

We typically process deletion requests within 7–30 days, depending on complexity and legal obligations.

Verification

To protect your data, we may request verification before deletion (e.g., confirming control of the email/workspace).

Questions

For any questions, contact: privacy@avra.example
